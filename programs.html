Admission Information

Undergraduate Admission

  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes
  • Equivalent qualifications from recognized institutions
  • Mature age entry for candidates 25+ years with work experience

Postgraduate Admission

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum GPA requirements
  • Professional experience (for some programs)
  • Research proposal (for research degrees)

Program Offerings

Detailed course information and requirements

Bachelor of Business Administration

Undergraduate

Duration: 3 years (full-time) | 4 years (evening/weekend)

Core Courses

  • Principles of Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Economics
  • Marketing Management
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Business Law & Ethics
  • Strategic Management
  • Entrepreneurship Development

Admission Requirements

2 principal passes at A-Level, or Diploma in Business or related field with at least second class lower.

Career Opportunities

Management roles in corporate, NGO, and government sectors; entrepreneurship; consulting.

Master of Business Administration

Postgraduate

Duration: 2 years (part-time available)

Core Courses

  • Advanced Financial Management
  • Strategic Leadership
  • Research Methods
  • International Business
  • Operations Management
  • Business Analytics

Specializations

  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • Human Resource Management
  • Entrepreneurship

Admission Requirements

Bachelor's degree with minimum second class lower; at least 2 years work experience preferred.

BSc Computer Science

Undergraduate

Duration: 4 years

Core Courses

  • Programming Fundamentals (Java, Python, C++)
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Admission Requirements

2 principal passes including Mathematics; or relevant Diploma with credit.

Career Opportunities

Software development, systems analysis, database administration, IT consultancy.

BSc Software Engineering

Undergraduate

Duration: 4 years

Core Courses

  • Software Design & Architecture
  • Web Development
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Quality Assurance & Testing
  • DevOps & Cloud Computing
  • Project Management
  • User Experience Design

Admission Requirements

2 principal passes including Mathematics or Physics.

Career Opportunities

Software engineer, full-stack developer, technical lead, product manager.

Bachelor of Nursing Science

Undergraduate

Duration: 4 years (including clinical practice)

Core Courses
