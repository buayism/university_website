Our History

The Department of Computing and Informatics was established in 1998 as part of Bugema University's commitment to providing quality technology education. For over 25 years, we have been at the forefront of computing education in East Africa, preparing students to become leaders in the digital economy.

From Humble Beginnings

What started as a small computer training unit has grown into a comprehensive department offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, Data Science, and Cybersecurity.

Growth and Innovation

Over the decades, the department has expanded its academic offerings, research capabilities, and industry partnerships. We have consistently adapted to meet the evolving needs of the tech industry while maintaining our commitment to academic excellence.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide quality computing education that develops technically competent, ethically grounded, and innovative professionals who can solve real-world problems through technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computing education and research, producing graduates who drive technological innovation and digital transformation in Africa and beyond.

Our Values

  • Excellence in teaching and research
  • Integrity and ethical computing practices
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Lifelong learning
  • Social responsibility through technology
