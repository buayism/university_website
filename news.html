March 10, 2026

Faculty Wins Research Excellence Award

The research team from the School of Science and Technology has been honored with the East African Research Excellence Award for their groundbreaking work on sustainable agriculture practices. The award recognizes their innovative approaches to helping smallholder farmers adapt to climate change.

Project: "Climate-Smart Agriculture for Smallholder Farmers in Uganda"

Lead Researchers: Dr. Charles Ssekandi and Prof. Peter Otim

March 5, 2026

New Computer Lab Inaugurated

Bugema University has officially opened a state-of-the-art computer laboratory equipped with 100 high-performance workstations. The facility will support teaching and research in computer science, data science, and artificial intelligence programs.

The lab features specialized software for programming, design, and simulation, along with high-speed internet connectivity and 24/7 access for students.

February 28, 2026

International Partnership Agreement Signed

Bugema University has entered into a partnership agreement with several European universities to facilitate student exchange programs, joint research initiatives, and faculty development opportunities.

The partnership will enable 50 students annually to participate in exchange programs and will support collaborative research projects in sustainable development and digital innovation.

February 20, 2026

Health Sciences Students Complete Community Outreach

Over 200 nursing and public health students recently completed a successful community health outreach program in rural districts of Uganda. The program provided free health screenings, health education, and referrals to over 5,000 community members.

This initiative demonstrates Bugema University's commitment to service learning and community engagement as part of our "Excellence in Service" motto.
