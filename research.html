OUR PROJECTS COLLABORATE

Research Areas

Key research focus areas in computing and informatics

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Research on deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and AI applications in healthcare, agriculture, and education in Africa.

Cybersecurity & Information Security

Studies in network security, ethical hacking, digital forensics, cryptography, and cybersecurity frameworks for African contexts.

Data Science & Big Data Analytics

Research on data mining, predictive analytics, big data technologies, and data-driven decision making for business and government.

Software Engineering & Systems

Studies in software design, quality assurance, DevOps, cloud computing, distributed systems, and mobile application development.

Human-Computer Interaction

Research on user experience design, accessibility, interaction design for African users, and technology adoption in developing contexts.

Computing Education

Studies on effective pedagogy in computing, curriculum development, online learning, and increasing diversity in tech education.

Ongoing Research Projects

AI for Healthcare in Africa

Duration: 2024-2027

Developing AI-driven diagnostic tools for rural healthcare facilities. Funded by the African Innovation Foundation.

Lead: Dr. Grace Achieng (Computing) & Dr. James Mbabazi (Health)

Sustainable Agriculture Initiative

Duration: 2025-2028

Research on climate-smart agriculture practices for smallholder farmers in Uganda. Partnership with Ministry of Agriculture.

Lead: Dr. Charles Ssekandi (Science)

Digital Financial Inclusion Study

Duration: 2025-2027

Examining mobile money adoption and financial literacy in rural communities. Collaboration with Financial Sector Deepening Uganda.

Lead: Dr. Robert Mukasa (Business)

Teacher Professional Development Program

Duration: 2024-2026

Evaluating effective models for in-service teacher training in East Africa. Supported by UNESCO.

Lead: Dr. Esther Nakato (Education)
