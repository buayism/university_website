    How to Find Us

    By Road

    Bugema University is located 32 kilometers from Kampala on the Gayaza-Zirobwe Road. The campus is easily accessible by both public and private transport. Look for the prominent university signboard along the main road.

    From Kampala

    Take the Gayaza Road from Kampala city center. Continue through Gayaza town for approximately 20 km until you reach the university entrance on your left. The journey typically takes 45-60 minutes depending on traffic.

    Public Transport

    Matatus (taxi vans) and buses heading to Zirobwe pass by the university gate. Board at the Kampala taxi park or Wandegeya stage. Inform the conductor you're heading to Bugema University.

    Campus Shuttle

    The university provides shuttle services from Kampala to campus on specific days. Contact the Student Affairs office for the current schedule and pick-up points.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I apply for admission?

    You can apply online through our website or visit the Admissions Office in person. Required documents include academic transcripts, ID/passport, and passport photos. Applications can also be submitted via email to admissions@bugema.ac.ug.

    What are the tuition fees?

    Tuition fees vary by program. Undergraduate programs range from UGX 1.5M to 2.5M per semester, while postgraduate programs range from UGX 2.5M to 4M per semester. See the Programs page for specific details or contact the Finance Office.

    Are scholarships available?

    Yes, Bugema University offers various scholarships including academic excellence awards, need-based support, and sports scholarships. Contact the Student Affairs Office or visit our scholarships page for eligibility criteria and application procedures.

    Do you offer part-time/evening programs?

    Yes, several programs are available in the evening and weekend formats to accommodate working professionals. These are primarily in Business, Education, and Computing programs. Check individual program pages for availability.

    Is accommodation available on campus?

    Yes, we offer on-campus accommodation in our student hostels. Both shared and single rooms are available. Accommodation is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Contact the Accommodation Office for rates and availability.

    How can I verify my certificate/transcript?

    Certificate and transcript verification requests should be directed to the Academic Registrar's Office via email to registrar@bugema.ac.ug or by calling +256 312 351 415. Processing typically takes 3-5 working days.
