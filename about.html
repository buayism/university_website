About Bugema University

Discover our rich heritage, mission, and commitment to excellence in education and service.

Our History

Bugema University was established in 1948 with a vision to provide quality Christian education in East Africa. For over 75 years, we have been committed to the motto "Excellence in Service," preparing students to become leaders in their communities and professions.

From Humble Beginnings

What started as a small training institution has grown into a comprehensive university with seven schools, serving thousands of students from across Africa and beyond. Our journey reflects dedication to academic excellence and spiritual development.

Growth and Expansion

Over the decades, Bugema University has expanded its academic offerings, research capabilities, and community impact. We have consistently adapted to meet the evolving needs of our students while maintaining our core values of integrity, service, and excellence.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide quality Christian education that develops the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—empowering graduates to serve humanity with excellence and integrity.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of academic excellence and research innovation, recognized globally for producing transformative leaders who impact society positively.

Our Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Service to God and humanity
  • Respect for diversity
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Environmental stewardship
